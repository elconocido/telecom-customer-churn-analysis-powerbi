# 📉 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard** developed in **Power BI** to help business leaders identify customer attrition drivers, quantify revenue leakage, and develop data-driven retention strategies.

The dashboard integrates customer, demographic, service, and geographic data to provide actionable insights through interactive visualizations and business recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses, directly impacting revenue and long-term profitability.

This project aims to:

- Identify the major drivers of customer churn.
- Quantify revenue lost due to customer attrition.
- Discover high-risk customer segments.
- Analyze geographic churn patterns.
- Provide actionable retention recommendations.

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

---

# 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of customer churn performance.

### KPIs

- Total Customers
- Active Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate %
- Total Revenue
- Revenue Lost
- Average Satisfaction Score
- Top Churn Reason

### Key Insights

- Customer churn rate stands at **26.54%**.
- Customer attrition resulted in approximately **$3.68M revenue loss**.
- **Month-to-Month customers** account for the majority of churn.
- Customers using **Fiber Optic services** exhibit the highest churn levels.
- The leading churn reason is **better competitor device offerings**.

### Business Recommendations

✅ Introduce incentives to migrate customers toward long-term contracts.

✅ Launch loyalty programs and device upgrade initiatives.

✅ Investigate pricing competitiveness and service quality of Fiber Optic packages.

---

# 2️⃣ Customer Segmentation Analysis

Analyzes churn behavior across customer demographics.

### Key Insights

- Highest churn is observed among customers aged **60+**.
- Customers with **no dependents** are significantly more likely to churn.
- **Unmarried customers** represent a larger share of churn.
- Gender has minimal impact on churn behavior.

### Business Recommendations

✅ Develop retention programs specifically for senior customers.

✅ Create personalized offers targeting single customers.

✅ Introduce family bundles and referral incentives.

---

# 3️⃣ Geography & Retention Strategy

Evaluates customer churn patterns across different locations.

### Key Insights

- Revenue leakage is concentrated in a relatively small number of cities.
- Population size alone does not explain churn behavior.
- Several medium-population cities exhibit disproportionately high churn.
- Geographic churn patterns suggest local market conditions influence retention.

### Business Recommendations

✅ Prioritize retention campaigns in high revenue-loss cities.

✅ Conduct regional competitor and service quality assessments.

✅ Develop city-specific customer engagement strategies.

✅ Allocate marketing budgets based on churn risk and revenue impact.

---

# 4️⃣ Service & Contract Analysis

Investigates how services and contract types influence customer churn.

### Key Insights

- **Month-to-Month contracts** drive most customer attrition.
- Customers paying through **Electronic Check** show the highest churn levels.
- Customers without promotional offers are more likely to leave.
- Fiber Optic customers contribute significantly to revenue leakage.

### Business Recommendations

✅ Encourage automatic payment methods through incentives.

✅ Improve Fiber Optic customer experience.

✅ Increase promotional offers for high-risk customers.

✅ Promote migration to long-term contracts.
